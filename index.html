EVOLUTION OF BASKETBALL

The Evolution of the Game. The first official game of basketball was played on January 20, 1892 in the same YMCA gym where it was invented. The contest featured two teams of nine players and ended in a score of 1-0—needless to say, Naismith’s game was far from the current version of basketball played worldwide today.
